About Us

Our mission is to build healthy digital public spaces and contribute to a safer and more inclusive web.

Founded in Bahrain in 2006, Majal has built a wide range of platforms that serve marginalized populations in the Middle East and North Africa. Majal primarily addresses issues that receive little or no attention: From rights of the invisible lower income migrant workers in the Gulf, to the stigmatized Aab LGBTQ+ community, to those seeking to express dissent through music in a region where censorship and surveillance remain the norm.

After a decade of local impact, credibility, and creativity, the scope of our work now goes well beyond the region, and we still have much more to do.
